Tournament Information

How much time does a game last?

A game has a total of 90 minutes in two halves, First half lasts 45 minutes, and the Second half lasts 45 minutes. The team with the highest points at the end will win the match

How many games does a team play?

Each team plays 8 matches, Each has to play with all the other teams and according to the points table the first two positioned teams will play Semi final-1 and the next two teams will play semi final-2 and the winners of both semi-finals will play Finals.
